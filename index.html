Hi! I'm Manu.

Currently: a backend software engineer, dabbling in DevOps and the frontend. Formerly: a graphic designer.

I believe design and software engineering are closely related, like Yukihiro Matsumoto (a.k.a Matz) once said: Don't underestimate the human factor. Even thought we are in front of computers, they are media. We are working for human, with human.

One of the things makes me passionate about software engineering is how projects can be almost living things, ever-evolving and changing.
